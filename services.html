Services

  • Graphic design

    whether you want a one-off logo render or a complete set of conceptual artwork for a project we can facilitate your designer needs. Using a blend of 2D and 3D rendering software in combination with videofx programs we can create almost anything. Using multiple programs including but not limited to Photoshop, PaintoolSAI, Blender, Cinema4D and SonyVegas. Our artists will work with you throughout the project to make sure you're satisfied with the designs every step of the way making use of constant feedback to properly develop the project.

    Pricing: Simple sketches can be comissioned for as low as £5, increasing in value with the level of work required. 3D logo renders usually costing in the region of £30.

  • Web / application development

    Web development for applications or websites, written using HTML5, CSS, Javascript, C#, SQL, JSNode, python, php. Ranging from desktop applications to online websites. Ask for a quote and we'll see if we can develop your idea together.

    Pricing: Depends on the specifications of the program required but as coding takes a lot of time to do anything worth any value starting prices are higher comparative to other services at £150, again scaling with the ammount of time needed to complete.

  • Custom PC build

    Completely custom PC builds, you can pick any available part to go into a system and we will keep you updated throughout the entire build process to make sure you recieve your dream rig. With over 4 years experience with configuring systems and fabrication there are many completely custom options we can implement into your next PC.

    Pricing: £35 deposit on all custom PC orders, price of custom PC build reflected by selection of parts going into the system.

  • Guitar / bass upgrades and repair

    We can upgrade the electronics and other components in your musical instruments to improve their feel and sound quality and can offer repair / maintenance services to keep your instrument in the best condition. If you want different pickups, a complete re-wire with custom parts of you have a custom finish in mind for a makeover then please feel free to ask for a quote.

    Pricing: Electronics upgrades / modifications cost considerably less than woodworking upgrades / modifications due to the nature of the work being carried out. Custom pickups can be installed for as low as £15 minus the cost of the pickup itself.

  • Misc.

    There are also a lot of unofficial services which we would be happy to carry out, this can range from general electronic installation to low-level civil engineering projects (ie; installing a pool) so please do not hesitate to enquire with a unique request as we may very well be able to assist you.
