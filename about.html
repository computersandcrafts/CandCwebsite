About Us

Computers and Crafts is an engineering company based in the south of Wales, United kingdom. What started off as hobbitual engineering work on a wide range of items from guitar repair to PC system maintanence soon grew into a full established business with a grand total of two employees. The initial focus of the company was to provide the specific details on performance regarding second hand items sold on various marketplaces, mainly the performance of systems / hardware in relation to their brand new counterparts providing peace of mind for potential buyers who would then be able to validate the performance reducing any risks within the second hand market.

Established in August 2018, Computers and Crafts expanded into more markets looking to bring good value to consumers while maintaining a strong environmentally friendly approach to industry.

We're hoping to further expand our media outlets and improve our relationship with the industry leaders, allowing us to bring you more information to better help understand the market, provide exceptional quality service and grant us the tools to bring future products to market.

What we do

There are numerous channels of work taken on by the company, with a background in traditional engineering disciplines and years of freelance designer experience the company expanded to accomadate the varying services that were available. Computers and Crafts now deals with 2D artwork, 3D artwork, intermediate program development, graphic design, musical instrument repair and customisation, computer repairs, system configurations, hardware refurbishment, complete-builds, low-level mechanical and civil engineering tasks, fabrication and many more...
